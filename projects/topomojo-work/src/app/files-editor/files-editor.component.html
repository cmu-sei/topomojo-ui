Files
Upload ISO files (or regular files) to include in your lab. Uploaded files can be selected as ISOs and attached to VMs in the Templates tab or attached dynamically per-variant in the Challenge tab. If your upload isn't already an ISO, TopoMojo wraps it into an ISO after upload.
Tip: With the Local filter enabled, you'll only see ISOs available to this workspace. Disabling it shows global ISOs shared across all workspaces.

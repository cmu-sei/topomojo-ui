Workspace

Have an idea for a lab or challenge? Create a workspace and get it configured.

Finally, publish your workspace so that others can launch it.

Gamespace

A gamespace is an instance of a lab or challenge in Play mode.

HotKeys

        Ctrl-O: open workspace
        Ctrl-L: toggle left panel
        Ctrl-H: navigate home
        ----
        Ctrl-S: workplace settings
        Ctrl-T: workspace templates
        Ctrl-D: workspace document
        Ctrl-C: workspace challenge
        Ctrl-F: workspace files
        Ctrl-P: play workspace
        Ctrl-[1-6]: navigate to workspace section
